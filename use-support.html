SUPPORT

During the hypercare phase it is important to highlight and offer the support that partnering with Zoetis gives. Long-term success in dairy production requires partners who drive efficiency, profitability and welfare. Proving that, our colleagues provide an industry leading level of support to our customers, directly on farm, to ensure the seamless adoption of enhanced capabilities whilst ensuring the smooth running of ongoing operations.

As part of the Continuum of Care, farmers have become an increasingly important target group for Zoetis alongside veterinarians. With a portfolio of genomic testing, sensor technology and diagnostic tools for cattle, Zoetis is now offering meaningful and future-proof products for farmers that perfectly complement the ruminant product portfolio. To enable the bringing of these solutions to our farm customers, our colleagues need to understand the needs and ways of working of a farm and how the Zoetis portfolio can fulfil these needs and overcome farmers’ challenges. The Direct to Farm strategy is a business model that enables us to bring the entire Continuum of Care to life, to add value and become a trusted partner to all customers. Therefore, a holistic training package, focussing on on-farm KPIs, success factors, daily routines, product specific information and solution selling, is available for all colleagues in the Precision Livestock Farming area.

Regular contact with in-market support is critical, and knowledge of the remote 24/7 monitoring and customer service that is available, will help give our new customers peace of mind that they have made the correct decision in choosing SMARTBOW and partnering with Zoetis. It is important to remember that support is proactive and not reactive.
