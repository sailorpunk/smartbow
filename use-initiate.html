INITIATE

The next phase in the customer journey is what sets Zoetis and SMARTBOW apart from the competition, the support and consultancy that can be offered by our in-market expertise to drive the direct to farm and continuum of care strategy.


Hypercare is the period of time immediately following a solution “Go Live” where an elevated level of support is available to ensure the seamless adoption of new capabilities. This would be supported by a local on Premise Team working with named Customer Support Team members to ensure smooth transition to ongoing operations.

Regular contact and support in the post purchase or “hypercare” phase can help us transition a customer into an advocate.

Put into action a market specific hypercare plan, including timelines on: training, contact, system health, usage. Engage on-farm partnership plans to ensure the benefits of this new relationship go beyond SMARTBOW and PLF, but help Zoetis drive the direct to farm Continuum of Care strategy.
